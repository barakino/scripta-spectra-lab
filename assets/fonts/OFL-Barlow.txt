Why it's here: the live Wix site sets headings/name/nav in "DIN Next W01 Light",
a proprietary font that cannot be redistributed. Barlow is an OFL-licensed,
DIN-adjacent geometric grotesk used here as a license-clean visual substitute.
You may swap in a licensed DIN Next if you own one — see wix-clone/css/style.css
(the `--din` variable and the @font-face block).
